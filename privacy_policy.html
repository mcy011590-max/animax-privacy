Privacy Policy

AnimaX Studio
Last updated: June 25, 2026

1. Introduction

AnimaX Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application AnimaX Studio handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

AnimaX Studio is a local animation app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

All data created in AnimaX Studio — including animation projects, frames, audio files, and exported videos — is stored locally on your device only.

3. In-App Purchases

AnimaX Studio offers a one-time Pro unlock purchase processed through Google Play Billing. We do not handle or store any payment information. All payment processing is handled by Google Play. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle payment data.

4. Permissions

AnimaX Studio may request the following permissions:

  • Storage / Media: To save your animation projects and export videos to your device
  • Audio: To import audio files for use in your animations
  • Camera (optional): For future reference image features

These permissions are used solely to provide app functionality and are not used to collect personal data.

5. Third-Party Services

AnimaX Studio uses Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. This service is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

AnimaX Studio is suitable for users of all ages including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your animation projects are as secure as your device itself. We recommend using device lock screens and keeping your device software updated.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
